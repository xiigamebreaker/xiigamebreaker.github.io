Frequently Asked Questions

We offer a variety of services including grooming, playroom access, and feeding options to ensure your cat's well-being and happiness during their stay with us.

We prioritize the safety of all cats in our care. Our facility is equipped with secure enclosures, and we have trained staff who monitor the cats at all times. We also follow strict health and hygiene protocols to prevent the spread of illness.

Our operating hours are from 8 AM to 8 PM, Monday through Saturday. We are closed on Sundays to allow our staff to rest and to perform thorough cleaning and maintenance of our facilities.
